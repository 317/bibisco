1. Have a deep relationship with your characters

Novels only work if their characters are believable.

Characters are believable when you can understand their human nature's complexity: qualities, defects, contradictions.

Characters that are completely good or completely evil are stereotypes and therefore are not believable.

In order to create believable characters, you have to deeply know everything about them: physical aspect, behavior, psychology, sociology, ideas, passions, life before the beginning of the novel.
